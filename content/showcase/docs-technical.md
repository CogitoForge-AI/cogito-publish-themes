**Docs Technical** is a handbook theme modeled on Google's docsy layout: a top
navbar, a left navigation sidebar that maps your content tree, an "on this page"
table of contents, breadcrumbs, prev/next links, and backlinks.

It is the right choice for product documentation, a knowledge base, or any site
where readers move between learning and looking things up. Pair it with the
`docs` preset, organize content into directories, and cross-reference pages with
`[[wikilinks]]`.
