**Blog Minimal** is a quiet, reading-focused blog theme. It puts your words
first: generous typography, a single column, and just enough chrome to navigate.
A light/dark toggle is the only flourish.

It is a good fit for a personal blog or a writing-heavy site where the design
should get out of the way. Adopt it by copying the theme directory into your
site and pointing `Config.layout` at it, then write Markdown under
`content/posts/`.
