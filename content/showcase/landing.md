**Landing** is a one-page marketing site for a product, app, or company: a
centered hero with a headline and call-to-action buttons, a grid of features, a
numbered "how it works" walkthrough, and a closing CTA band -- all assembled from
a single Markdown file's frontmatter. A generic inner-page template covers
pricing, about, or terms pages.

It is an original PySSG theme (hand-written CSS, Inter typography) inspired by the
common product-landing archetype rather than a port of a specific theme.
