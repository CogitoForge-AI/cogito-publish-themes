Driftless itself is **free and open source**. There is no paid tier, no seat
count, and no usage meter -- you run it on your machine and in your CI.

## What it costs

| Plan | Price | For |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Open source | Free | Everyone. The whole tool, forever. |
| Hosting | Your host's price | You pick the static host; we don't resell it. |
| Support | Community | Issues and discussions on GitHub. |

## Why free

A static site generator is a build tool, not a service. It produces plain files
and then gets out of the way, so there is nothing to bill for. If Driftless
saves you time, a star on the repository is thanks enough.
